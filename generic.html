About
Daniel Evans

Below is a brief overview of my life's work

Ever since I was young I've been intrigued with creating something new and different. My intrest in pursuing this started with film and television. Going to a Savannah College of Art and Design gave me an ability to create complex short and long form videos using film and video. While attending SCAD i began my career in local new broadcast with WTOC-TV as an editor of nation/local news for the most viewed shows of the day. After fulfilling my goal of becoming a full-time videographer with WTOC-TV, I continued to hone my skills as a creator thru challenging myself with 24 hour short-film contest and working on freelance jobs.
