About
Daniel Evans

Below is a brief overview of my life's work

At a young age I've been intrigued with creating something new and different. My interest in pursuing this started with film and television. Going to a Savannah College of Art and Design gave me an ability to create complex short and long form videos using film and video. While attending SCAD I began my career in local news broadcasting with WTOC-TV, as an editor of nation/local news for the most viewed shows of the day. After fulfilling my goal of becoming a full-time videographer with WTOC-TV, I continued to hone my skills as a creator thru challenging myself with 24 hour short-film contests and working on freelance jobs.

From Oct. 2021 to present I've refocused from videography to computer science and coding with the goal of securing a new career path in software programming/developing and data analytics. Course work ranging from learning courses, webinars, and self-guided instructions. My ultimate goal is to secure a career-oriented position in a company with growth potential where I can utilize my wide set of skills and knowledge in Information Technology and Computer Science to add value to the business. Additionally, my goal is to expand my abilities and expertise to further myself and move up within a company.
